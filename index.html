Overview

What is YouTube TV?

YouTube TV is a live TV streaming service from Google that replaces your traditional cable subscription. It delivers live broadcast and cable channels directly over the internet — no antenna, no satellite dish, no cable box required.

📺

Live Television

Watch sports, news, and entertainment live, just like traditional cable TV.

☁️

Cloud-Based DVR

Record unlimited shows with no storage limits, and replay them for up to 9 months.

📱

Watch Anywhere

Stream on your phone, tablet, computer, smart TV, or gaming console.

👨‍👩‍👧

Family Sharing

Add up to 5 family members, each with their own personal library and recommendations.

Setup Guide

How to Connect YouTube TV

Getting started takes less than 5 minutes. Follow these simple steps and you'll be watching live TV in no time.

🔍
Step 01

Check Availability

Visit tv.youtube.com and enter your ZIP code to confirm YouTube TV is available in your area. Coverage is available across most of the United States.

📧
Step 02

Sign In with Google

Use your existing Google account (Gmail) to sign in, or create a free Google account at accounts.google.com. No new username or password needed.

💳
Step 03

Choose Your Plan

Select a subscription plan. A free trial is often available for new members. Enter your billing information to activate your subscription.

📲
Step 04

Download the App

Download the YouTube TV app on your preferred device from the App Store, Google Play, or your smart TV's app marketplace.

🔑
Step 05

Sign In to the App

Open the app, sign in with the same Google account you used to subscribe, and your channel lineup will load automatically.

🎉
Step 06

Start Watching!

Browse live channels, set up your DVR recordings, and customize your home screen. You're ready to cut the cord!

Pro Tips

Get the Most Out of YouTube TV

Insider tips to supercharge your YouTube TV experience from day one.

1

Set Up Your DVR Library First

As soon as you sign in, add all your favorite shows and teams to your DVR library. YouTube TV will automatically record every new episode and game.

2

Use the Live Guide for Sports

The Live tab shows everything airing right now. Use the filter for Sports to quickly find what games are on without scrolling through all channels.

3

Enable Parental Controls

Each family member's profile can have content ratings restrictions. Set up a PIN-protected content lock in Settings → Family sharing to keep kids safe.

4

Cast to Your TV with Chromecast

Chromecast is the easiest and cheapest way to get YouTube TV on your big screen. Simply tap the Cast icon in the app and select your TV.

5

Fast-Forward Through Ads on DVR

When watching DVR recordings, you can fast-forward through commercials using the 15-second skip button for a smoother viewing experience.

6

Lower Data Usage on Mobile

In the app Settings, switch to "Data saver" or "Auto" quality when on a mobile network to reduce data consumption without sacrificing too much quality.

7

Check the "Top Picks For You" Row

YouTube TV learns your preferences over time and suggests content you're likely to enjoy. Check this row on the home screen regularly.

8

Pause & Resume on Different Devices

Started watching on your phone? Pause, then resume on your TV seamlessly. Your watch progress syncs across all your devices automatically.
